Davis – Team lead

Automated deal intake

⚠️

Deal intake is manual and inconsistent

Pitch decks land in a shared inbox as PDFs, PowerPoints, or DocSend links. Analysts manually triage, rename, file, and summarize each deck, a process that can take 30+ minutes per deal and is prone to duplication and missed follow-up.

✅

Davis automates the entire intake

Davis monitors the inbox, detects and deduplicates submissions, files to a standardized Google Drive structure, and uses an LLM to generate a concise prescreen brief and fit score. It then routes the deal to the right investment manager, no manual steps required.

Five agentic capabilities

  • Email inbox monitoring (PDF, PPTX, DocSend)
  • Deduplication by SHA-256 + fuzzy matching
  • Standardized Google Drive filing & naming
  • LLM pre-screening on market, vertical and focus area
  • Sector-based manager assignment & notifications

Morgan – Cap table agent

Automated cap tables

⚠️

Cap tables are built manually from scattered documents

SAFE notes, CLAs, equity issuances, and option pool schedules sit in separate Drive folders. Building and maintaining a fully diluted cap table means reading each document, interpreting conversion terms, and reconciling holders manually – a high-effort, error-prone process with real LP and board risk.

✅

Morgan reads, interprets, and normalizes automatically

Morgan ingests cap documents from Google Drive, classifies each instrument, extracts holder and term data using an LLM, and applies standard conversion formulas to produce a fully diluted cap table. Ambiguous clauses are flagged for human review before the table is finalized.

Five agentic capabilities

  • Ingest SAFEs, CLAs, equity docs, option pool
  • LLM extraction of holders, terms and instruments
  • Calculate fully diluted ownership % computation
  • Flag ambiguity in documents and/or clauses
  • Export to Excel
